Radiology Film Print Management System
Castle Street Hospital for Women, Ministry of Health, Nutrition and Indigenous Medicine, Sri Lanka

- For a meaningful implementation of PACS



 The Radiology Film Print Management System was developed with the intention of promoting film-less radiology at Castle Street Hospital for Women while providing film printing capability through this paperless request system for ordering films for uses such as transferring of patients outside the hospital, teaching purposes or legal purposes.
